What to Look For
1
Look for document types: Lis Pendens, Final Judgment, Claim of Lien, IRS Lien, Code Enforcement
2
Check dates — look at records from the last 10 years
3
If you see a lien, look for a matching Release of Lien or Satisfaction — that means it was paid off
4
Open lien with no release = call a title company before you do anything else
🟢 No open liens → clean, proceed with confidence
🟡 Small HOA or code lien → negotiable at closing
🔴 Large judgment or IRS lien → title company call before any offer
