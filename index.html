What to look for

1

Document types: Lis Pendens, Final Judgment, Claim of Lien, IRS Lien, Code Enforcement.

2

Check dates — read records from the last ten years. Older usually doesn't matter.

3

If you find a lien, look for a matching Release of Lien or Satisfaction. That means it was paid off.

4

Open lien with no release? Stop. Call a title company before doing anything else.

No open liens — clean, proceed with confidence.

Small HOA or code lien — usually negotiable at closing.

Large judgment or IRS lien — title company before any offer.
